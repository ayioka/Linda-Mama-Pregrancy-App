About Linda Mama

Empowering mothers with technology-driven maternal healthcare solutions

Our Mission

To revolutionize maternal healthcare in Africa by providing accessible, reliable, and personalized support to every expectant mother.

Linda Mama was born from the recognition that many preventable pregnancy-related complications occur due to limited access to timely medical information and support. We bridge this gap through technology.

Our Story

From vision to reality

2023

The Beginning

Founded by a team of healthcare professionals and technology experts who witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by expectant mothers in accessing quality healthcare services.

2024

Pilot Launch

Successfully launched our pilot program in three counties, serving over 1,000 mothers and establishing partnerships with local healthcare facilities.

2025

National Expansion

Expanded our services to 15 counties across Kenya, developing innovative features like emergency alert systems and AI-powered health monitoring.
